Welcome to Bosnia

Bosnia is a country of beautiful nature and rich history and culture. Bosnia is a country that has known the world for the last 1,000 years, and some sources say it has existed since the 7th century, under its name Bosnia, and it was only by annexation of the Austro-Hungarian Empire in the 19th century that it was given a continuation of Herzegovina, all for the sake of treacherous political influences to divide it more easily, in the time that comes afterwards and eventually prove it. Bosnia is a country where 4 seasons change, and 2 climate zones, mediterranean in the south and continental in the north. Bosnia is mainly a hilly country, with a lot of sums, rivers, lakes and waterfalls. In Bosnia, it manages a variety of fruits and vegetables, so in the north manage plum, apples, pear, peach, cherry, raspberries, blackberries, honey while in the south succeed grapes of olive pomegranate tobacco, mandarins. It is also important to say that bosnia is the only rainforest in Europe that is europe's lungs. In short, Bosnia is a land of many miracles, natural and cultural, various cultures, very rich history, which it has taken on both from the east and the west. Dobro došli u Bosnu

Bosna je zemlja prelijepe prirode i bogate historije/povijesti i kulture. Bosna je drzava koja je svijetu poznata zadnjih 1000 godina, neki izvori navode da je postojala jos od 7 stoljeća, pod svojim imenom Bosna, a tek je aneksijom Austrougarske Imperije u 19 stoljeću nove ere dobila nastavak Hercegovina, sve zarad izdajnickih političkih utjecaja da bi se u vremenu koje dolazi poslije lakše podijelila a na kraju dokinula. Bosna je zemlja u kojoj se mjenjaju 4 godišnja doba, te 2 klimatske zone, mediteranska na jugu i kontinentalna na sjeveru. Bosna je uglavnom brdovita zemlja, sa puno šuma, rijeka, jezera i vodopada. U Bosni uspijeva raznoliko voće i povrće, tako na sjeveru uspijevaju šljive jabuke kruške breskve višnje trešnje,maline, kupine, med dok na jugu uspijevaju grožđe maslina nar(šipak) duhan, mandarine. Takođe bitno je kazati da se u Bosni nalazi jedina prašuma u Evropi koja su pluća Evrope. Ukratko Bosna je zemlja mnogih čuda, prirodnih i kulturoloških, raznih kultura, prebogate historije/povijesti, koje je poprimila kako sa istoka tako i sa zapada. Uvijek će te primiti, ali nikad se nije dala niti se da pokoriti.
